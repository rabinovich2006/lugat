Dictionary illustrations

The 726 illustrations in the children's picture dictionary were extracted from the original 2019 print volume Balaçıqlar İçün Resimli Luğat by Veli Bakalov. They are reproduced here with the author's permission for this online edition.

Text content

The biographical entries in the original Crimean Tatar language are taken from Resimli Luğat. English summaries and historical context were written for this site, drawing on standard academic references (Williams, Uehling, Allworth) and primary documentation cited in the History timeline.
